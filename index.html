STEP 3: Daily Update ▸
What this does:
  1. Decreases happiness by 3% per active war (max 15% per day regardless of war count)
  2. Increments war day counters by 1 for all active wars
  3. Decrements truce day counters by 1; removes expired truces
  4. Applies subsidy bonuses (+5% × eco pts) and increments subsidy day counters
STEP 4: Weekly Update ▸
What this does (in order):
  1. Food −1% per military pt; stability −1% per economic pt (happiness handled by daily update)
  2. Applies export bonuses to food, stability, and happiness
  3. Export-driven pop stats consume eco income at a 1:0.25 conversion rate (e.g. 4% food from exports costs 1 eco income)
  4. Adds province income (econ & mil) for provinces ≥50% owned
  5. Resets defence to the province def-income total
  6. Applies trade node penalty if applicable
  7. If econ > 50, decays excess by 10%; if mil > 50, decays mil by 10%
